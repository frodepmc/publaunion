Our history and commitment to your night out.

Our History

Since 2016, La Unión Pub has been the meeting point for those looking to extend the night until dawn in Playa de Palma. We were born from the idea of being more than just a bar: a refuge. A place where you choose the music, conversations flow, and there's always an excuse for one more toast. We are the last bastion of the night, the place you come to when you don't want the fun to end.

The Team

Behind the bar and at the door, you'll find people passionate about the night. We are not just bartenders; we are hosts. We take pride in knowing our customers by their name and their favourite drink. Our team is trained to ensure not only fast service but also a welcoming and safe environment for everyone.

Safety and Conduct Policy

Your fun is our priority. Your safety, our responsibility.

Zero Tolerance for Drug Use and Sale

At La Unión Pub, we have a zero-tolerance policy towards the use or sale of any illegal substances. Our security and bar staff are instructed to act immediately upon any suspicion. We want our establishment to be a safe and clean space, where the only addiction is to good music and a great atmosphere.

Anyone who violates this rule will be immediately removed, and the relevant authorities will be notified. We have CCTV systems in place to ensure compliance with this policy and the safety of all our customers.
